The Cost of Disorganization and Low Engagement

Global workforce engagement is at a worrying low. Gallup’s 2025 State of the Global Workplace shows engagement dropped to 21%, costing the world economy $438 billion in lost productivity. Managers saw the steepest decline, dropping from 30% to 27%.

As a result, low engagement and messy systems mean:

  • Slow execution

  • Missed opportunities

  • Burned-out teams

On the flip side, companies that build better systems and invest in employee happiness see dramatic returns.

Clear Systems = More Profit, Less Stress

Companies with streamlined workflows report:

Happy Teams Perform Better

Happy teams propel growth.
On the contrary, stressed and disengaged teams drag everything down.
